In Magusterra there are many sources of energy that one can attune with/draw on to power their magic. One of the more potent sources of magic that can be drawn on by the inhabitants of Magusterra are the World Trees. There are 5 world trees in Magusterra, each having its own primary school of magic that it energizes. 
1. [Terrginnan](/magusTerraWiki/worldtrees/terrginnan/)
1. [Terrkanei](/magusTerraWiki/worldtrees/terrkanei/)
1. [Terrliiff](/magusTerraWiki/worldtrees/terrliiff/)
1. [Terrsang](/magusTerraWiki/worldtrees/terrsang/)
1. [Terrcadere](/magusTerraWiki/worldtrees/terrcadere/)
