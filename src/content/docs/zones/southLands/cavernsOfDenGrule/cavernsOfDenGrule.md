The Caverns of Den Gru'le is a hidden cave in the southern reaches of MagusTerra, is where the events of the game [Discovering Olivia Northrop](/magusTerraWiki/videogames/escape/) take place, and it is inhabited by a warlock cult who kidnaps villagers to sacrifice them.
